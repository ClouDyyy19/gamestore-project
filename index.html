What is sonic?

Sonic the Hedgehog is the main character of the Sonic the Hedgehog video game series

from Sega, as well as the comic books, animated series and feature films created on its basis.

Sonic is a blue hedgehog. Sonic got his name for his ability to run at supersonic speeds.

The gameplay for Sonic in most games in the Sonic the Hedgehog series consists of quickly

completing levels and fighting enemies, to attack which Sonic curls up into a ball while jumping.

An important role for Sonic is played by golden rings, which serve him as protection.
